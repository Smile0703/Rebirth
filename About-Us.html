TEAM UNITE

Hello everyone! We are Team Unite! We are students from Tunku Abdul Rahman Universiti College. We are currently studying in multimedia design. Before we officially graduate, the Final Year Project is the largest and indispensable project. This project not only shows what we have learned in the past, but also highlights our abilities and attitudes. *Rebirth* is a horror and decryption 2D game. Horror games often give people an illusion just to experience the horror. In fact, this is wrong. In various horror games and movies, it is more to express the true tragedy. This game is also based on the same concept. This original script will make you feel one of the tragedy in our life.
